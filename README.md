# Sports Science Analytics Reports

As a Sports Science & Analytics Intern with the University of Delaware, I developed match analysis reports that helped evaluate team performance, identify tactical trends, and support coaching decisions. My primary focus was offensive and defensive analysis, including shot-location mapping, shot-on-target evaluation, and opponent comparison.

Offensive analysis involved mapping shot coordinates and categorizing each shot by outcome, such as goal, on target, or off target. These visualizations helped identify attacking patterns, shot trends, and areas where the opposition defense was most vulnerable. I also calculated expected goals (xG) from shot coordinates and applied the same framework to opponent data for direct team comparisons.
